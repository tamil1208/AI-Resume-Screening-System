# Flagship Execution Plan (Tech Roles)

## Phase 1: Strong MVP Foundation (completed)

- FastAPI backend scaffold
- Explainable ranking output
- Skill taxonomy for tech hiring
- Unit test baseline
- venv-based setup and run scripts

## Phase 2: Recruiter-Ready Intelligence (implemented)

- Parse PDF, DOCX, and TXT resumes into normalized text
- Skill weighting by role family (backend, frontend, data_ai, devops, fullstack)
- Hard constraints (must-have skills)
- Nice-to-have scoring bands
- Stronger explainability in score response fields

## Phase 3: Premium Product UX

- Dashboard for JD upload and batch resume upload
- Candidate leaderboard with score breakdown visuals
- Candidate-vs-candidate comparison panel
- Export shortlist reports to CSV and PDF

## Phase 4: Trust and Production Signals

- Configurable anti-bias guardrails and score transparency
- Authentication and project-level job history
- Persistent storage (SQLite/PostgreSQL)
- Audit logs for screening decisions

## Phase 5: Upwork Portfolio Packaging

- Case study with problem/approach/outcome
- 60 to 90 second demo video
- Screenshots and feature highlights
- Service tiers (basic, standard, premium)
- Proposal bullets tailored to HR and startup clients

## Success Metrics

- Resume screening throughput: 100 resumes under 3 minutes (target)
- Match quality: maintain consistent top-5 relevance on benchmark set
- Explainability coverage: every score includes evidence and gaps
